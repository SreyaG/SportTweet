Tweet-lete

Mining Sentiment in Twitter data to Observe the Role of Gender in Tweets about Athletes

Social media has been a boon to data analysis, allowing data scientists to directly collect data from individuals, with Twitter leading at the forefront with more than 500 million tweets a day for analysis. I intend to mine this data to observe the role of gender in in individual opinion about sport personalities. I will analyze tweets to understand sentiment associated with male vs female athletes. I will also study the regional context of this data. This study has a dual impact: picked up

  1. this information allows brands to appropriately chose brand-ambassadors to regions where impact of a certain sports personality is higher; and schedule sporting events.
  2. mproved understanding of gender bias in sport and possible remedial actions.
