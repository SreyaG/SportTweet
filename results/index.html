Results


Observations I

The number of countries tweeting about atheletes, when divided by gender, is starkly different, with 60 countries tweeting about male athletes while only 26 countries tweeted about female athletes, i.e. only 43% of the countries that tweeted about male athletes also tweeted about female athletes. The total number of tweets for female athletes was 36,141 while for male it was 131,181, i.e. only 21% of all the tweets were about women. The distribution of tweets based on country is shown below.
All tweets did not have information about their location so a smaller subset was used.


Observations II

Tweets associated with male athletes showed a dominating 61.1% of neutral sentiment, followed by a 33.6% of positive sentiment and 5.3% of negative sentiment. Female athletes too showed 48.1% of positive sentiment, followed by 44.7% of neutral sentiment and finally 7.2% negative sentiment. This initial investigation shows that female athletes get more of both positive and negative opinions.

But there was a caveat, with Serena Williams announcing her pregnancy, a lot of tweets congratulated her on her motherhood. I took out data for Serena Williams (who is the highest paid female athlete in the world) and new relations came to light with negative tweets regarding female athletes increasing to 12.0% (4.8% increase) reducing positive tweets to 40.8% and increasing neutral tweets to 47.2% respectively. For comparision I filtered out tweets for Cristiano Ronaldo who is the highest paid male athlete, which introduced a smaller increment of only 0.2% in negative tweets, i.e. the new data-set had only 5.5% negative tweets and 33.7% positive tweets and 60.8% neutral tweets, which is an insignificant amount of change. This highlights the harsher criticism towards female athletes than their male counterparts.


Observations III

A study of sentiment of tweets athletes further classified by region. USA has the highest number of tweets for both male and female athletes. India, Nigeria along with countries rated higher in gender equality namely USA, UK, and Germany show positive sentiments with regards to female athletes.

Observations IV

An observation of the timelines of the tweets show, most tweets irresective of countries, are tweeted at nights.
